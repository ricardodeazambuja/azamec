Nossos projetos são desenvolvidos, desde a ideia inicial, através de modernos softwares de projeto e simulação - tudo em 3D. Portando fica fácil pra gente mostrar porque nossos equipamentos não têm similares no mercado mundial.

Cut to Lenght Line with Angular Shear

Automatic cut to lenght line with angular cuts executed in halt mode, but with continous feeding from straightener, better surface quality and production throughput.

Angular Flying Shear

One more time leading, Azamec presents your Angular Flying Shear, for thickness up to 13mm. With a flying shear system, is possible to reach 50% more productivity and, due to angular cuts, a better raw material utilization. This equipment is one choice of shear to be used in ours cut to lenght lines.

Hot Rolling Mill - Light Profile - AZA-LPL/16

The Hot Rolling Mill (light profile) AZA-LPL/16 has a continuos hot rolling process, so interruptions occurs only on bar type change, working with only two passes and producing profiles up to 1,27 kg/m feeded with wire rolls with diameter from 6 to 16mm. Reduced investment and transformation costs are the key feature our equipment. The product production range includes flats bars, angles, squares, T-bars and several other according to customer needs. Besides, the same technology and concepts applied in this mill could be applicable to larger equipments.
